We're Done Playing Small

ElevateHer exists because women shouldn't have to figure out their careers alone.

The Real Story

ElevateHer started from a frustration we've all felt: sitting in another "women in tech" panel where senior leaders share polished success stories—but never the messy, tactical truth about how they got there.

We wanted something different. Not a networking event where you collect business cards and never talk again. Not a mentorship program that pairs you with someone who's "too busy" to meet.

We wanted a space where senior women could share the real playbook—the negotiation scripts that actually worked, the career moves they'd make differently, the politics they navigated.

And we wanted junior and mid-level women to have a real voice too. Because wisdom flows both ways.

Our Values

These aren't corporate buzzwords. They're how we actually operate.

Radical Honesty

We don't sugarcoat. We share what actually works, what doesn't, and what we wish someone had told us earlier.

Generosity Over Competition

We reject the lie that there's only room for one woman at the top. When one of us rises, we all rise.

Action Over Talk

Inspiration is nice. But we're here for templates, scripts, frameworks, and tactical advice you can use tomorrow.
